    It’s cold and things but things are hot. I bet y’all are stressin’ too. Tryna decide whether to go protest our shitty justice system or if you should finish binding your book about cupcakes. Just be careful not to burn yourself out. Channel your fire. Everything passes with time and the decision to do nothing is a decision in itself. Follow your light and keep your fire burning at a steady pace, drink lots of water, breathe, and do what you have to do. Oh also, listen to this playlist I put together. It’s real hot. It’s stressful but, hopeful and full of life. These tracks are ideal for getting pumped while designing, dancing, yelling, protesting, boxing, demonstrating in a public square, etc.
